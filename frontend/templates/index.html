How Recall works

One card, on a schedule that adapts to you.

1

Add a card. It's due for review immediately — you'll see it the first time you open the Review tab.

2

First review is fixed. Whatever you rate it the first time (as long as it's not "Again"), it comes back tomorrow — no exceptions on review one.

3

From then on, your rating decides the pace:

Again

You forgot it. Progress resets completely — the card comes back tomorrow, as if it were new.

Hard

You struggled. The card's "ease" drops slightly, so it will keep coming back more frequently until it gets easier.

Good

You remembered it normally. The gap before the next review grows steadily (e.g. 1 day → 6 days → ~15 days...).

Easy

It was effortless. The card's "ease" increases, so it will show up later and less often — you've basically mastered it.
